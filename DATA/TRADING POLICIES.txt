# Blue Box + Marubozu Trend Continuation — Active Entry Policies

Signals fire **once per closed candle** only. Data: **Bybit LINEAR klines**.

## Blue Box trap (state machine)

### Bullish trap → LONG (BB-L)
1. Sweep: `low < lowest_20` AND `close > lowest_20`
2. Displacement: next 1–2 bars green, `body ≥ 2× expected_body`

### Bearish trap → SHORT (BB-S)
1. Sweep: `high > highest_20` AND `close < highest_20`
2. Displacement: next 1–2 bars red, `body ≥ 2× expected_body`

## Momentum (MOM-L / MOM-S)
Green or red candle with `body ≥ 2× expected_body` (no sweep required).

## Marubozu trend continuation (MBZ-L / MBZ-S)

### Trend filter (50 & 200 EMA)
- **Uptrend:** EMA50 > EMA200 AND close > EMA50
- **Downtrend:** EMA50 < EMA200 AND close < EMA50

### Marubozu candle
- `body > avg_body_20 × 1.5`
- `upper_wick ≤ body × 5%` AND `lower_wick ≤ body × 5%`

### Pullback + trigger
| Code | Trend | Pullback (prior 2–4 bars) | Trigger |
|------|--------|---------------------------|---------|
| MBZ-L | Uptrend | Mostly red (retracement down) | Green marubozu |
| MBZ-S | Downtrend | Mostly green (retracement up) | Red marubozu |

## Execution & risk (all patterns)
- **Entry:** signal candle close
- **SL:** pattern-specific extreme ± 0.1% buffer (marubozu uses trigger candle high/low)
- **TP:** fixed **1:2 R:R**
- **Size:** 1% of balance risked to SL
- Priority: Blue Box displacement → momentum → Marubozu
